About

Welcome to Record Reversi, where you can play your friends for the ultimate battle of who is the better DJ! You can DJ battle against your friends or any other random DJs throughout the world. When you invite another DJ to play, they can accept your invite. After accepting your invite, both of you will be taken to the game page where your game of Record Reversi will start. The player who entered the lobby first will be assigned to play the black records and will begin the game. A blinking square will highlight which moves you are allowed to make. The DJ who "mixes" the most records at the end of the game, aka whichever one of you has the most of your color records on the board, wins! You may exit the game at any time. Best of luck!
