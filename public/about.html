About

This is a web-based version of the game Reversi. You can play against your friends or any other random players throughout the world. When you invite another player, they can accept your invite. After accepting your invite, both of you will be taken to the game page where your game of Reversi will start. The player who entered the lobby first will be assigned to play the black tokens and will begin the game. A yellow and black square will highlight which moves you are allowed to make. The game ends when one of the players runs out of moves or the board becomes completely full. The player with the most tokens on the board at the end wins. You may exit the game at any time. Best of luck!
